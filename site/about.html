Jessica Merritt
Design Direction & Client Relationships

Most agency websites look like other agency websites. Most dental websites look like other dental websites. Jessica is why ours don't.

She studied fine art and design formally — the kind of training that teaches you to see proportion, hierarchy, and tension before you ever open a design tool. She spent years teaching that same eye at UCLA. Now she uses it to make sure every business we work with looks like it belongs in a different category than its competitors.

She also handles every client relationship at BLDR. Which means when you call, she picks up. When something needs explaining, she explains it. No ticketing systems. No account managers. Just her.
